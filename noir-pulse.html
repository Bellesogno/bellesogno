✦  The Story
"You don't just wear a scent. You become one."

Developed in a Parisian atelier over eighteen months, Noir Pulse draws from the tension between restraint and electricity. The master perfumer described it as 'the scent of a decision made.' Every note was chosen not for beauty alone, but for psychological impact — the way Rhubarb signals arrival, Red Apple deepens the encounter, and Sandalwood ensures you're never truly forgotten.

✦  The Art of Wearing

How to Wear Noir Pulse

01
The Entrance

Apply to pulse points — wrists, neck, the inner elbow — 15 minutes before you arrive. Let the opening notes settle into your skin's warmth before you walk in.

02
The Encounter

For close encounters, a single drop behind the ear. Noir Pulse is designed for proximity — its heart note reveals itself only to those you allow near.

03
The Exit

Spray lightly on the back of your hair. As you move, the base note diffuses — leaving a trail of Sandalwood that lingers in rooms you've already left.
