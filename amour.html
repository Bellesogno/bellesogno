✦  The Story
"Love is the only thing worth wearing."

Amour was written in the language of restraint. Where other fragrances announce themselves, Amour confides. The Cardamom opening was chosen for its paradox — warm yet precise, familiar yet electric. As Sage settles in, the fragrance softens into something you want to stay inside. And the Vanilla base? It was not added for sweetness, but for memory. Scent and emotion share the same neural pathway. Amour was engineered to be unforgettable.

✦  The Art of Wearing

How to Wear Amour

01
Before the Evening

Apply 20 minutes before your date. Amour's opening note is its most electric — give it time to warm into your skin before the first impression.

02
For Closeness

The heart note is designed for intimacy. Apply sparingly — a touch at the collarbone, a breath on the wrist. Amour rewards those who come close.

03
The Morning After

Amour's Vanilla base has exceptional longevity. A single evening application carries into the following morning — a quiet reminder of the night before.
