✦  The Story
"Some light doesn't blind. It simply reveals."

Lumen began as a question: what does confidence smell like when it has nothing to prove? The answer took two years and forty iterations. Blood Mandarin was chosen for its precision — not sweet, not sharp, but exactly right. The Rose heart rejects nostalgia entirely; it is rose for people who don't think they like rose. And the Leather base was the final revelation: grace with a spine. Lumen is for those who already know who they are.

✦  The Art of Wearing

How to Wear Lumen

01
The Daily Ritual

Lumen was made for mornings. Apply after your shower while skin is still warm — the Blood Mandarin opening pairs perfectly with freshly cleansed skin.

02
The Office Presence

A professional scent should command respect, not distract. Two sprays — one at the wrist, one at the neck — is all Lumen requires. It does the rest.

03
Layering

Lumen's clean structure makes it ideal for layering. Apply it over an unscented moisturiser for amplified projection, or layer with Amour for a rose-vanilla depth.
